“Resilient lines” and colorful interactive wall niches, or pods are dominant features of this two-level office for an organization which promotes disaster resilient buildings and infrastructure. The “resilient lines” here represent elasticity or “the ability to come back”. These lines are converging and diverging at various pressure points and spreading across the reception area and the informal lounge. The colorful pods follow a color psychology and thus have spaces accordingly. They are monochrome in their theme and all the furniture in those pods match their respective color. The informal lounge is dominantly on the entry level floor to have an energetic welcome.
